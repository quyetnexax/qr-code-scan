Privacy Policy for Quick QR Scanner

Effective Date: January 11, 2026

Quick QR Scanner ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our QR code scanner application.

Information We Collect

Camera Permission:
The app requires camera access to scan QR codes and barcodes in real-time. Camera data is processed locally on your device and is not stored, transmitted, or shared with any third parties.

Image Access:
When you choose to upload an image containing a QR code, the app accesses the selected image from your device storage. The image is processed locally and is not stored, transmitted, or shared.

Data Storage

Scan History:
The app stores a local history of scanned QR codes on your device for your convenience. This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is not transmitted to any servers
  • Can be cleared at any time from the app settings

No Personal Information:
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers

Data Usage

All data processing occurs entirely on your device:

  • Camera feed is used only for real-time QR code scanning
  • Scanned content is displayed to you and optionally saved locally
  • No data is sent to external servers or third parties

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any information with third parties.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and we do not transmit or store data on external servers, your information is protected by your device's security measures.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with a new effective date.
