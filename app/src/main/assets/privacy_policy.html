Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2026

Introduction

QR Scanner ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our QR Scanner application.

Information We Collect

Camera Access: Our app requires camera access to scan QR codes and barcodes. We only use your camera to process images locally on your device for scanning purposes. We do not store, transmit, or share any images or camera data.

How We Use Your Information

The camera permission is used solely for:

  • Scanning QR codes and barcodes in real-time
  • Decoding the information contained in the scanned codes
  • Displaying scan results to you

Data Storage

All scan history and data are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. You have full control over your data and can clear your scan history at any time through the app settings.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share your information with any third parties.

Data Security

We are committed to protecting your data. Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's settings or support channel.

Permissions Explanation

Camera Permission: Required to scan QR codes and barcodes using your device's camera.

Vibration Permission: Used to provide haptic feedback when a code is successfully scanned.

Internet Permission: Used only when you choose to open web links from scanned QR codes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your scan history stored on your device
  • Delete your scan history at any time
  • Revoke camera permissions through your device settings
  • Uninstall the app, which will remove all locally stored data

By using QR Scanner, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
